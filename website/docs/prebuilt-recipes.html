Pre-built Recipes

The extension comes with pre-configured recipes for popular websites. When you visit a supported site, the matching recipe is automatically suggested.

RecipeFieldsPagination
Amazon Search ResultsProduct Name, Price, Rating, Image, URLClick Next (5 pages)
Google Search ResultsTitle, URL, SnippetClick Next (5 pages)
YouTube Search ResultsTitle, Channel, Views, URLNone
Hacker NewsTitle, URL, Points, AuthorClick Next (5 pages)
GitHub RepositoriesRepository, URL, Description, LanguageClick Next (5 pages)

Pre-built recipes are a great starting point. You can duplicate and customize them to fit your exact needs.
