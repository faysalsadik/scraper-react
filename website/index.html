Not at all. Simply point and click on the data you want to extract. Our visual selector makes it easy for anyone to scrape websites without writing a single line of code.

What websites can I scrape?

You can scrape virtually any public website — e-commerce stores, directories, job boards, social media profiles, news sites, and more. If you can see it in your browser, you can scrape it.

What formats can I export data in?

You can export your scraped data as CSV, JSON, or Excel files. This makes it easy to import into spreadsheets, databases, or any other tool you use.

Is web scraping legal?

Scraping publicly available data is generally legal. However, you should always respect website terms of service and avoid scraping personal or sensitive information.
